FAR DPF CHALLENGE

LIQUID PROPELLANT ROCKET TEAM

ARA's FAR DPF Team aims to break the 23,000 ft collegiate liquid propellant rocket altitude record and become the 8th university team in history - 1st among the SEC universities - to successfully achieve liquid propellant rocket flight. TheFAR DPF or "Dollar-Per-Foot" Challenge awards a dollar for every foot off the launch rail to the student team thatsuccessfully launches their liquid propellant rocket.
2022 by Alabama Rocketry Association.
